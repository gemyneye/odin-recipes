Pizza


Description

We all love a good pizza and the recipe below is simple and delicious.

Just follow the recipe below for a great pizza. You can add your own toppings, just keep in mind if you add more toppings, you may have to adjust cooking time.

Ingredients

  • Pre-made Pizza crust
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese (or your favorite)
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Open crust and spread the sauce evenly. Not too thick as it could create a mess.
  2. Sprinkle cheese evenly over the pizza.
  3. Top with as much pepperoni as you wish.
  4. Bake according to crust instructions. Hint: look at the crust for a golden brown color (not black).
