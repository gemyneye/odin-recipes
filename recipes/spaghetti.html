Spaghetti


Description

Spaghetti is one of those Italian staples and we can make a super fast simple version of it.

You could call below a 'recipe' if you wanted to, but it's more of instructions on how to make a quick meal and leftovers. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • One Box of spaghetti (Your choice of style. I prefer Angel Hair)
  • One Jar of spaghetti sauce (can if you are on a tight budget)
  • One Onion (white or yellow)
  • One lb Ground Beef
  • Optional Avocado oil

Steps

  1. Preheat pan of oil.
  2. When warm add chopped onion and ground beef.
  3. Once beef is brown, pour sauce in the pan and allow to simmer on low
  4. While sauce is simmering, bring water to boil for noodles per instructions on the box.
  5. Once noodles are done to your liking, drain and return to pot and pour sauce with the noodles. Mix well.
  6. Top with Parmesan Cheese (or cottage... try it, you might like it).
