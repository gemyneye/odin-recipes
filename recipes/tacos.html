Tacos Today


Description

We have all heard of Taco Tuesday, but why limit when you can have tacos?

Here we have a 'recipe' that is about as simple as we can make it; and you can whip it up after work with minimal time and cleanup. Keep in mind the more toppings you have, the longer the cleanup.ðŸŒ®

Ingredients

  • One lb ground beef
  • One packet taco seasoning
  • Avocado Oil
  • Premade/formed taco shells
  • One Onion
  • Toppings (or choose your own)
    • Red pepper
    • Jalapeno pepper
    • Sour cream
    • Avocado
    • Shredded Cheese
    • Salsa (Jar or store made)

Steps

  1. Chop the onion and other toppings as needed
  2. Preheat frying pan and add Avocado oil
  3. Add onion and ground beef and cook til browned
  4. Follow packet instructions to add to browned beef
  5. Preheat oven and warm taco shells per box instructions
  6. Remove taco shells and add all the toppings you want
  7. Eat and enjoy!
